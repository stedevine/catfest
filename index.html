What is this?

It's four awesome bands playing in an amazing venue to support Seattle Area Feline Rescue.

Is that some kind of cat charity?

It's a 503(c) non-profit no-kill cat rescue organization that finds homes for cats and kittens.

Can you be more specific about this so called "raising money?"

Yes! 100% of the door (that's insider speak for ticket sales) will go to Seattle Area Feline Rescue. It cost $250 to run the event (sound, security, etc.) and Straight To Video are covering that, so all the ticket sales go to SAFe Rescue.

And will there also be cats?

Well, no. Cats generally don't like loud music. But there will be a lot of cat lovers in attendance, and we will be raising money for a great cause.

Or should you say great paws?

No. We're not doing cat puns.
